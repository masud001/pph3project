Our company

Talent2celeb.com was created to connect talents to opportunities in the fashion and entertainment industry. We work with industry partners to provide development opportunities and top casting calls for talent members on our platform. Casting and industry professionals can directly Post Auditions and Jobs or Post Broadcast Notice on the platform and these listings are shared with talent members and on our social media channels.

We made it easier for casting and industry professionals to post auditions and cast new and experienced faces. Our application system matches auditions and jobs posts with talents to ensure only qualified talents can apply. With our "Application Box Technology" casting is easier and more cost effective.

Protecting Talents

Talents are advised to be extremely careful and avoid exchanging contact details with shady clients who might come at you with offers too good to be true or even good to be true but have other intentions. We strive to keep these crooks off our platform however, it is essential for talents to be extra careful. If in doubt send us a message at support@talent2celeb.com and we will conduct a private investigation about the client and get back to you as soon as possible in most cases within 24hrs.

We don’t just publish a job listing. We ask questions, check references, and do not publish casting calls or jobs that appear less than reputable. Our job listings are verified to be from companies with known contact information. Although it’s not common for approved listings to be fraudulent, however, a few might slip through hence we insist talents inform us if in doubt of client’s motive or if activities are in doubt. We believe a doubt is enough fraud hint, so if you are in doubt please get in touch with us.

You should never go into an audition or job listing if you are not sure of the client motive. Do not pay any client for any audition.

If a notice or a listing on talent2celeb.com is actually a scam or deliberately misleading, please send us a mail AS SOON AS POSSIBLE as we aim to deal with such matters seriously. Our contact mail: support@talent2celeb.com
